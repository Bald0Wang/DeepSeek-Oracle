# Risk Grading

## S0 Normal
General emotional support and non-critical guidance.
Action: allow.

## S1 Major Life Decisions
Marriage/divorce/resignation/major relocation.
Action: allow with `light` disclaimer and non-deterministic language.

## S2 Finance
Stocks/funds/crypto/leverage/debt/gambling.
Action: rewrite; remove trading commands; keep risk education only; `strong` disclaimer.

## S3 Medical or Mental Crisis
Diagnosis, prescriptions, self-harm hints.
Action: rewrite to professional help recommendation; if self-harm risk is explicit, refuse and provide emergency-seeking guidance.

## S4 Illegal or Harmful Acts
Violence, crime, attacks, malicious manipulation.
Action: refuse.

Language constraints:
- Prefer: "可能" "更适合" "建议考虑"
- Forbid: "一定" "必须" "不做就会出事"
